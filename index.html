About

The data logic is universal.
The domain expertise is rare.

SeesamiData Consulting is a boutique data science practice founded by Mai Nguyen — PhD in Integrative Life Sciences, bioinformatician, and former data scientist at BCPlatforms. Based in Helsinki, working across Europe.

Like the sesame seed — small, ancient, full of hidden value. We specialise in unlocking what's buried in complex biomedical datasets: from liquid biopsy pipelines to real-world evidence for regulatory submissions.
